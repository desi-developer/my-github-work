About Us

Clear Lotus Publishing was founded in 2007 by Dusty Blackwell, a publishing entrepreneur who believes that the success of a book depends on the following three main factors:

1. The quality of content
2. Marketing and promotion expertise, and
3. Access to the “means” of publishing

Clear Lotus is dedicated to providing independent authors with a feasible alternative publishing channel where they would get a trade quality publishing experience but at a fraction of the time, money and effort if they did entirely on their own. Modern digital printing technology and web based distribution channels are combined here with the expertise of many highly qualified publishing professionals.
